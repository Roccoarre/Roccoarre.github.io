    Who I am

    Ab0ut

    I work in sound design and visual identity for fashion and music: from runway shows to campaigns, from backstage footage to more experimental projects with 3D scanning and animation.

    I've worked closely with students and faculty at Iuav University of Venice, and with textile companies such as Bonotto and Stoll Italia, shaping the visual narrative of collections and production processes.

    I believe in a clean aesthetic, never loud: the work should speak for itself, not distract from it.
